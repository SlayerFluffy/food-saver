How Food Saver Works

Food Saver is meant as a means to save time and money by helping you find recipes based on ingredients you already have in your house. This is done through a simple process.

1

Search Ingredients

Search for ingredients you already have or need to use, but don't know what to do with.

2

Find Recipes

Food Saver will find recipes by searching the Spoonacular API for recipes including your list of ingredients.

3

Save & Plan

You can then save those recipes, make a meal plan based on what you find, or compile a shopping list for the (hopefully few) ingredients you are missing for the recipe(s) you are interested in.

This will help enable you to make delicious meals, use up what's already in your pantry, and spend less by only buying what you need.
